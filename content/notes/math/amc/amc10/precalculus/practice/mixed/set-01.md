<details><summary>Answer & Solution</summary>

<p>We have $a_{n+1} + 1 = 2(a_n + 1)$. Let $b_n = a_n + 1$. Then $b_{n+1} = 2b_n$ with $b_1 = 2$. So $b_n = 2^n$, giving $a_n = 2^n - 1$. Therefore $a_5 = 2^5 - 1 = 32 - 1 = 31$.</p>
</details>
