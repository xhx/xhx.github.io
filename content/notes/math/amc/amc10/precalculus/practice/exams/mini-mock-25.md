### 22.
*Tags: Complex Powers · Hard · source: Original (AMC-style)*

What is (1 + i)⁴?

A) $1$
B) $-1$
C) $i$
D) $-i$
E) $-4$

<details><summary>Answer & Solution</summary>
<p><strong>Answer: E</strong></p>
<p>(1 + i)² = 1 + 2i + i² = 1 + 2i - 1 = 2i. So (1 + i)⁴ = (2i)² = 4i² = 4(-1) = -4.</p>
</details>
