That turns the probability into $$\boxed{;\displaystyle P =\frac{\text{favorable measure}}{\text{total measure}};}$$ with “measure” = area, length, or sometimes angle. Squares / rectangles Scale with side lengths; sub-rectangles drop out instantly. Triangles Slice with parallel lines → similar-triangle area ratios.
