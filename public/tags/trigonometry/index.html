Trigonometry Basics

📐 Trigonometry Basics Master the unit circle, special angles, and right triangle relationships. These fundamentals are essential for all AMC trigonometry problems. 🎯 Key Ideas Unit Circle: Circle with radius 1 centered at origin. Coordinates $(x,y)$ correspond to $(\cos\theta, \sin\theta)$. SOH-CAH-TOA: For right triangles: $\sin = \frac{\text{opposite}}{\text{hypotenuse}}$, $\cos = \frac{\text{adjacent}}{\text{hypotenuse}}$, $\tan = \frac{\text{opposite}}{\text{adjacent}}$.
