Xiaohui Xie is a full professor in the Department of Computer Science at UC Irvine, where he has been since 2007. He received his PhD from MIT, and completed his postdoctoral training at the Broad Institute of MIT and Harvard University.

He is passionate about AI/machine learning, neural networks, deep learning, and AI4Science. He lives in Irvine, California.
