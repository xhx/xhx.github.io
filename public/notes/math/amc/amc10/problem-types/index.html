游꿢 Detailed Problem Type Analysis#

游릭 Easy Problems (1-10)#

1. Divisibility Rules#

What to look for: Numbers that must be divisible by specific values Strategy: Apply divisibility rules directly Example: “Find the largest 4-digit number divisible by 36” Solution: Check divisibility by 4 and 9, then find largest such number

2. Prime Factorization#

What to look for: Expressing numbers as products of primes Strategy: Factor completely using small primes Example: “Find the prime factorization of 2024” Solution: $2024 = 2^3 \cdot 11 \cdot 23$

3. GCD & LCM#

What to look for: Common factors or multiples Strategy: Use Euclidean algorithm or relationship formula Example: “Find $\gcd(48, 60)$ and $\text{lcm}(48, 60)$” Solution: $\gcd(48, 60) = 12$, $\text{lcm}(48, 60) = 240$

游리 Medium Problems (11-20)#

4. Modular Arithmetic#

What to look for: Remainders, last digits, cyclic patterns Strategy: Find patterns, use Fermat’s Little Theorem Example: “Find the last two digits of $7^{100}$” Solution: Use Euler’s theorem: $7^{40} \equiv 1 \pmod{100}$

5. Linear Diophantine Equations#

What to look for: Integer solutions to $ax + by = c$ Strategy: Check $\gcd(a,b) \mid c$, find particular solution Example: “Find all positive integer solutions to $3x + 5y = 47$” Solution: $x = 4 + 5t$, $y = 7 - 3t$ for $t \geq 0$

6. Sequences#

What to look for: Patterns in number sequences Strategy: Identify type (arithmetic, geometric, recursive) Example: “Find the 100th term of the sequence 2, 5, 8, 11, …” Solution: Arithmetic sequence: $a_{100} = 2 + 99 \cdot 3 = 299$

游댮 Hard Problems (21-25)#

7. Chinese Remainder Theorem#

What to look for: Systems of congruences Strategy: Solve pairwise, combine solutions Example: “Find smallest positive integer $x$ such that $x \equiv 2 \pmod{3}$, $x \equiv 3 \pmod{5}$, $x \equiv 2 \pmod{7}$” Solution: $x = 23 + 105k$ for integer $k$

8. Advanced Modular Arithmetic#

What to look for: Complex exponentiation, large numbers Strategy: Use Euler’s theorem, find cycles Example: “Find $3^{1000} \bmod 1000$” Solution: Use Chinese Remainder Theorem with moduli 8 and 125

游 Problem-Solving Strategies by Type#

Divisibility Problems#

  1. Check divisibility rules - Start with 2, 3, 5, 9, 11
  2. Use prime factorization - Look for common factors
  3. Apply GCD/LCM relationships - Often key to solving

Modular Arithmetic Problems#

  1. Choose appropriate modulus - Based on what you’re finding
  2. Use Fermat’s Little Theorem - For prime moduli
  3. Apply Euler’s Theorem - For composite moduli
  4. Find patterns - Look for cycles in powers

Sequence Problems#

  1. Identify the type - Arithmetic, geometric, recursive
  2. Find the pattern - Look for common differences/ratios
  3. Use formulas - Apply appropriate sequence formulas
  4. Check with small cases - Verify your understanding

Diophantine Equation Problems#

  1. Check existence - $\gcd(a,b) \mid c$ for $ax + by = c$
  2. Find particular solution - Use Euclidean algorithm
  3. Write general solution - Add multiples of fundamental solution
  4. Apply constraints - Find solutions meeting given conditions
