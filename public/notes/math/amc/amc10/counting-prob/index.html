Principle of Inclusion-Exclusion (PIE) The Principle of Inclusion-Exclusion (PIE) is a fundamental counting technique in combinatorics used to calculate the size of the union of multiple sets. It helps avoid over-counting elements that are common to multiple sets by systematically including and excluding intersections.
