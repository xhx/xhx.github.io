📖 Topic Guides — Algebra Mastery#

Detailed guides for each algebra topic, organized for systematic study.

🎯 Core Topics (AMC10 Essential)#

🔢 Fundamentals#

  • Algebra Basics — Order of operations, factoring patterns, identities
  • Linear Equations & Inequalities — One-variable, interval notation, graph sense

🎯 Quadratic Systems#

  • Absolute Value — Piecewise setup, distance view
  • Quadratics & Parabolas — Vertex/axis, discriminant, completing square

🧮 Polynomial Theory#

  • Polynomial Theory — Division, remainder/factor theorems, Vieta

🔄 Rational & Radical#

  • Rational Expressions — Domains, asymptote sense, extraneous roots
  • Radicals & Exponents — Rational exponents, conjugate/rationalize

🎲 Systems & Advanced#

  • Systems of Equations — Substitution/elimination, 2×2, 3×3, nonlinear pairs
  • Inequalities & Optimization — Sign charts, AM-GM/Cauchy, bounds
  • Sequences & Recursions — Arithmetic/geometric, simple recurrences, telescoping

🚀 Advanced Topics (AMC12 Extensions)#

🧮 Advanced Algebra#

  • Functional Equations — Substitutions, symmetry, injective/surjective checks
  • Exponential & Log Equations — Equation/ineq solving, change-of-base
  • Complex Numbers — Algebra, quadratic roots, Arg basics
  • Equations with Parameters — Number of solutions via discriminant/degeneracy
