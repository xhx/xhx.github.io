Fraction Decomposition Fraction decomposition is a method of expressing a complex rational function as a sum of simpler fractions. This technique, commonly known as partial fraction decomposition, helps in integrating rational functions and solving algebraic equations, particularly in calculus and algebra. The general process involves two steps: Factor the denominator. Decompose the fraction into simpler terms according to the structure of the factors in the denominator.
