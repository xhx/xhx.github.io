What is the value of $1 + 2 + 3 + \cdots + 100$?

A) $4500$
B) $5000$
C) $5050$
D) $5100$
E) $5500$

Strategic Analysis

Estimation: Sum of first 100 positive integers. Use formula: $\frac{n(n+1)}{2} = \frac{100 \cdot 101}{2} = 5050$.

Expected Value: 1 choice remaining, EV = 6 points.

Answer & Solution

Answer: C

Using sum formula: $\frac{n(n+1)}{2} = \frac{100 \cdot 101}{2} = 5050$.

What is the value of $\sqrt{200}$?

A) $10$
B) $12$
C) $14$
D) $16$
E) $18$

Strategic Analysis

Estimation: $\sqrt{200} = \sqrt{2 \cdot 100} = 10\sqrt{2} \approx 10 \cdot 1.414 \approx 14.14$. So answer is around 14.

Expected Value: 1 choice remaining, EV = 6 points.

Answer & Solution

Answer: C

$\sqrt{200} = \sqrt{2 \cdot 100} = 10\sqrt{2} \approx 14.14$.
