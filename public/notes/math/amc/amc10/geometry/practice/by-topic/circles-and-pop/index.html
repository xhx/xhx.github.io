1.#

Tags: Geometry ¬∑ Difficulty (E/M/H) ¬∑ source: AMC10 2020 #1

What is the area of a triangle with base 6 and height 8?

A) $12$ B) $24$ C) $36$ D) $48$ E) $64$

Answer & Solution

Answer: B

The area is $\frac{1}{2} \cdot \text{base} \cdot \text{height} = \frac{1}{2} \cdot 6 \cdot 8 = 24$.

2.#

Tags: Geometry ¬∑ Difficulty (E/M/H) ¬∑ source: AMC10 2020 #2

What is the circumference of a circle with radius 5?

A) $5\pi$ B) $10\pi$ C) $25\pi$ D) $50\pi$ E) $100\pi$

Answer & Solution

Answer: B

The circumference is $2\pi r = 2\pi \cdot 5 = 10\pi$.

3.#

Tags: Geometry ¬∑ Difficulty (E/M/H) ¬∑ source: AMC10 2020 #3

What is the area of a circle with diameter 8?

A) $8\pi$ B) $16\pi$ C) $32\pi$ D) $64\pi$ E) $128\pi$

Answer & Solution

Answer: B

The radius is $\frac{8}{2} = 4$, so the area is $\pi r^2 = \pi \cdot 4^2 = 16\pi$.

4.#

Answer & Solution

Answer: C

5.#

Tags: Geometry ¬∑ Difficulty (E/M/H) ¬∑ source: AMC10 2020 #5

What is the surface area of a cube with side length 4?

A) $48$ B) $64$ C) $96$ D) $128$ E) $192$

Answer & Solution

Answer: C

The surface area is $6s^2 = 6 \cdot 4^2 = 6 \cdot 16 = 96$.
