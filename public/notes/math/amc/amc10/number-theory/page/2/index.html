Legendre's Formula

Legendre‚Äôs Formula Legendre‚Äôs Formula provides a way to count the exponent of a prime $p$ in the prime factorization of $n!$. Statement For a prime $p$ and positive integer $n$, the exponent of $p$ in $n!$ is: $$\sum_{k=1}^{\infty} \left\lfloor \frac{n}{p^k} \right\rfloor$$ In practice, we only need to sum until $p^k > n$, since $\left\lfloor \frac{n}{p^k} \right\rfloor = 0$ for $p^k > n$.
